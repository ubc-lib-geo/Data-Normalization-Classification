# Data Normalization & Classification

Learing Outcomes

1) Investigate how data normalization impacts the way we perceive patterns in a dataset

2) Look at different data classification methods and how they impact the way we perceive patterns in a dataset

What is Data Normalization

Normalization, is the process of scaling (AKA Normalizing) one number by another. For example, we can as the question: How many people do Canadian Police kill per year? We need two pieces of information:
A) The number of victims of police violence
B) The lenght of the period of record
Dividing A by B, this will tell us the average number of police killings per year over the period of record.

Data Classification Methods

We can use different classification methods to highlight different patterns in a dataset. The ones we'll focus on today are:
* Equal Interval
* Quantile
* Natural Break
* Standard Deviation
* Manual Breaks
